DEEPP — End-to-End Churn Analysis

A full workflow project: cleaning → EDA → insights → dashboard.

Python Tableau EDA

Objective

Identify what drives churn and translate it into actions that can realistically reduce churn.

Process

  • Prepared and cleaned data for analysis.
  • Explored churn patterns and key segments.
  • Translated findings into a Tableau dashboard for quick exploration.

Key Findings

  • Overall churn rate: 55.4%.
  • At 7× over-limit, churn is near-certain (99.8%).
  • Bundling is associated with lower churn (~42% lower).

Recommendations

  • Prioritize early warnings and interventions for customers approaching usage limits.
  • Offer relevant bundling options to reduce churn risk.
  • Monitor churn drivers through a consistent dashboard view.
